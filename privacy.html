Privacy Policy for Write Mate

This Privacy Policy describes how Write Mate collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. By using the application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name and email address collected through Firebase Authentication.
  • Profile Information: Bio, subjects, pricing, writing samples, and other profile details provided by users.
  • Uploaded Images: Users may upload images such as profile photos and images of notes or assignments. These images are hosted using the ImgBB image hosting service.
  • Location Data: Approximate GPS location used to help students find writers nearby.
  • Request Data: Information related to writing requests between students and writers.
  • Device Information: Basic device information used to maintain app functionality and improve performance.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • Provide and maintain the Write Mate service
  • Connect students with nearby writers
  • Allow users to send and manage writing requests
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Ensure security and prevent misuse of the platform

Location Information

Write Mate uses location data to display writers within a limited radius (approximately 4 km). This helps students discover writers nearby. Your location information is used only for this purpose and is not shared publicly without your permission.

Payments and Pricing Information

Write Mate does not process, collect, or manage any in-app payments. The application only acts as a platform that connects students with writers who can help with writing or note preparation.

Writers may display an estimated price or cost for their services within the application. This price may be shown using the Indian Rupee symbol (₹) only for informational purposes so that students can understand the approximate cost of the service.

All payments or financial agreements between students and writers occur outside the Write Mate application and are handled directly between the users.

Write Mate does not facilitate transactions, store payment details, or take any commission from these interactions. Therefore, Write Mate is not responsible for payment disputes, agreements, or transactions made outside the platform.

User Responsibility and Platform Disclaimer

Write Mate acts only as a platform to connect students with writers. The application does not verify, guarantee, or endorse the quality, accuracy, or reliability of services provided by users.

Students and writers are solely responsible for their communication, agreements, and any work or services exchanged between them.

Write Mate is not responsible for disputes, service quality issues, missed deadlines, or any agreements made between users outside the application.

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services that collect information used to identify you.

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore Database
  • ImgBB API (for uploading and hosting user images such as profile photos and notes)
  • Advertising networks displaying advertisements within the app

These third-party services operate under their own privacy policies and may collect certain information as required for their services. Images uploaded by users may be processed and stored by the ImgBB image hosting service.

Ad Blocker Detection

Write Mate may detect whether an ad blocker or similar technology is active on the device. This detection is used only to ensure the proper functioning of advertisements displayed within the app.

The ad blocker check does not collect personal information and is used solely to maintain the functionality of the app’s advertising services.

Data Retention

We retain user information only for as long as necessary to provide the service and fulfill the purposes described in this policy. If a user requests account deletion, their personal data will be removed from our active systems within a reasonable time unless retention is required by law or security purposes.

Data Security

We value your trust in providing your personal information and strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

Write Mate does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will immediately delete it from our systems.
