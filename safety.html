Writemate Child Safety Standards

Writemate is committed to maintaining a safe and respectful environment for all users. We strictly follow child safety laws and Google Play policies.

Zero Tolerance Policy

Writemate has a zero-tolerance policy toward any form of:

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)
  • Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)
  • Exploitation of minors
  • Any inappropriate or harmful content involving minors

Safety Measures

  • User account monitoring
  • Content review and moderation
  • Immediate removal of violating accounts
  • Permanent bans for serious violations
  • Cooperation with law enforcement authorities when required

Reporting Child Safety Concerns

Users can report any child safety concerns by contacting our support team directly. We take all reports seriously and act promptly.

📧 Contact Email: writemate.support@gmail.com

Compliance

Writemate complies with all applicable child safety laws and Google Play policies. We are committed to preventing abuse and ensuring a safe platform for everyone.
